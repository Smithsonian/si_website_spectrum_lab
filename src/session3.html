What can spectra tell us about an object’s temperature?

Stars are too far away (and too hot!) for a human to touch, yet we can measure their temperatures. Spectra make this possible! Let’s investigate how.

Session 3 Tutorial:

  1. What is the relationship between the wavelength and energy of light?
  2. Why might it be useful to display a spectrum graph as a scatter plot instead of a line chart?
  3. What parts of the electromagnetic spectrum are not displayed in the online Spectrum Tool?
